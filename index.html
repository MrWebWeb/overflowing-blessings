About Us

Our goal is to provide the right space for the right people. To build and develop properties for people to work in, to live in and to create meaningful moments together.

We are a family-run business. Our grandparents Patria Gaudiel and Federico Gaudiel were business people, but more than that they believed in people.

They created opportunities and dignified work for others. We want to continue the tradition of lifting others up through the blessings given to us in good measure, pressed down, shaken together and running over. That is what overflowing blessings represent.
